Introduction

This is the tertiary document members agree to adhere towards in the Forma Labs membership agreement. It contains minor or fluid-by-nature rules that may change without prior notice, but which are binding upon members nevertheless. The scope of these rules, where possible, will be as narrow as practiceable, keeping important changes to the other two scopes of the agreements.

Current Grattan House Rules

Left-Handed Witchcraft Forbidden

Please refrain from dark magic within the main or secondary lab, and in public spaces. Minor cantrips performed in private that do not affect others may be tolerated if sanctioned by a director in advance in writing. Sanction to engage in dark magic may be revoked at any time without notice.
